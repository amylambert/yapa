# YAPA - User Operational Guide

Welcome to YAPA. This guide outlines how to navigate, configure, and manage
your daily workspace routines, tracking project goals, tasks, and notes seamlessly.

## 1. Getting Started

- **Account Registration:** Navigate to `/accounts/register/` to create your
  isolated workspace profile.
- **Authentication:** Access your control frame via `/accounts/login/`. Your session
  persists dynamically until you choose to explicitly log out.

## 2. The Core Dashboard

The Dashboard serves as your centralized mission control center:

- **Google Calendar Integration:** Displays your live embedded calendar frames
  synchronizing schedule milestones directly.
- **Urgent Tasks Framework:** Displays the top 5 closest upcoming, incomplete
  tasks with valid future deadlines.
- **Recent Notes Feed:** Surfaces your 5 most recently updated internal notes
  or scratchpads for rapid context retrieval.

## 3. Managing Projects, Tasks, and Notes

YAPA runs on a cascading hierarchy designed to keep your workflows organized:

- **Projects:** The macro layer containing target milestones and overall timeline
  boundaries.
- **Tasks:** Granular action items containing functional progress states (`TODO`,
  `IN_PROGRESS`, `DONE`).
- **Notes:** Context documents supporting deep, multi-tiered nesting. You can build
  sub-notes inside parent notes or attach notes straight to tasks.

## 4. Inline Modifications & Custom Tags

- **Live Field Updates:** Click and modify titles, descriptions, priorities, or
  deadlines right on your active resource canvases. The fields update
  asynchronously without full page reloads.
- **Tagging Architecture:** Categorize your documents by adding comma-separated
  labels when creating notes to enable high-efficiency searching later.

## 5. Security Settings

- Locate the Account Settings panel to modify primary credentials.
- Update your administrative cryptographic security keys (Passwords) securely
  to maintain full account integrity.
